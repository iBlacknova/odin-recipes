Porkchops with Rice Recipe

One pot porkchops with rice!

Designed to make your mouth water. Quick and easy to make and goes down a treat

Ingredients

  • Porkchops
  • Rice
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Seasonings of choice
  • Bell Pepper
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Vegetable Broth

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Season Porkchops
  3. Boil Rice in vegetable broth
  4. Add porkchops on top of rice
  5. Bake in oven
